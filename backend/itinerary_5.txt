
Day 1:
* Arrive in Niagara Falls and check into your hotel.
* Visit the iconic Niagara Falls and take a guided tour of the area.
* Try some local street food, like hot dogs or poutine, at a restaurant in the Fallsview District.
* Visit the Niagara Falls History Museum to learn about the history of the region.
* Take a day trip to the nearby town of Niagara-on-the-Lake and try some local cuisine at a restaurant or winery.
* Return to Niagara Falls for dinner and enjoy some more local cuisine, like steak or seafood.

Day 2:
* Take a guided tour of the Falls and learn about its history and ecology.
* Visit the nearby Whirlpool Aero Car, a historic aerial car that takes you over the Niagara River.
* Take a cooking class and learn to make some traditional dishes, like peameal bacon or BeaverTails.
* Visit the Art Gallery of Ontario or the Royal Ontario Museum to see some local art.
* Take a language class or conversation exchange to practice the local language.

Day 3:
* Take a visit to the beautiful nature reserve of the Niagara Gorge.
* Take a hike along the gorge and enjoy the views.
* Visit the nearby town of Chippawa and try some local cuisine at a restaurant or market.
* Return to Niagara Falls for dinner and enjoy some more local cuisine, like steak or seafood.

Day 4:
* Depart from Niagara Falls

Note: This itinerary is a rough guide and can be adjusted to suit your interests and budget. It is always recommended to check the opening hours of the places you want to visit, and book tickets in advance if possible to save time.
