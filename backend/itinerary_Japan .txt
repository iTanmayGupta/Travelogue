
Day 1:
* Arrive in Tokyo and check into your hotel
* Visit the Imperial Palace and take a guided tour
* Explore the bustling shopping and food district of Shinjuku
* Try traditional Japanese BBQ for dinner

Day 2:
* Take the subway to the historic neighborhood of Asakusa and visit the Senso-ji Temple
* Explore the streets of the old town of Shitamachi
* Try some local street food

Day 3:
* Take a train to the city of Yokohama and visit the Nissan Global Headquarters
* Explore the city's famous Chinatown and try some local cuisine

Day 4:
* Take a train to the city of Osaka and visit the Universal Studios Japan theme park
* Try some of the park's many thrilling rides and attractions

Day 5:
* Take a train to the city of Kyoto and visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine
* Explore the city's historic temples and shrines
* Try some local cuisine at a traditional Japanese restaurant

Day 6:
* Take a train to the city of Kobe and visit the Kobe Harborland and the Kobe Museum
* Enjoy a farewell dinner of traditional Japanese cuisine

Day 7:
* Depart from Japan
