
Day 1:
* Arrive in Rome and check into your hotel.
* Visit the iconic Colosseum and take a guided tour of the building.
* Walk around the Roman Forum, a historic neighborhood with cobbled streets and 19th-century buildings.
* Visit the Museum of the Roman Empire and learn about the country's history.
* Try some local cuisine at a restaurant in the city center.

Day 2:
* Take a guided tour of the Vatican Museums and the Sistine Chapel.
* Visit the Saint Peter's Basilica and take a climb to the top for a panoramic view of the city.
* Explore the streets of the Trastevere neighborhood and try some local street food.

Day 3:
* Take a day trip to the Tuscany region and visit the city of Florence.
* Visit the Duomo and the Santa Maria del Fiore Cathedral
* Take a walk along the Arno River and try some local cuisine at a restaurant in the city center.
* Visit the Uffizi Gallery and see some of the city's famous artworks.

Day 4:
* Return to Rome and visit the Trevi Fountain and the Spanish Steps.
* Take a guided tour of the Pantheon and the nearby Church of Santa Maria Sopra Minerva.
* Visit the Campo de' Fiori market and try some local cuisine.

Day 5:
* Visit the Catacombs and explore the underground tunnels and tombs.
* Take a guided tour of the city and visit some of the famous landmarks, like the Colosseum and the Roman Forum.

Day 6:
* Depart from Rome
