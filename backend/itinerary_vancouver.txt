
Day 1:
* Arrive in Vancouver and check into your hotel.
* Visit the iconic Stanley Park, a large urban park with a variety of attractions and activities.
* Take a walk along the seawall and enjoy the views of the city and the North Shore.
* Visit the Vancouver Museum and learn about the city's history and culture.
* Try some local cuisine at a restaurant in the Gastown neighborhood.
Day 2:
* Take a guided tour of the city and visit some of Vancouver's famous street art.
* Try some local street food in the laneways.
* Go paragliding or bungee jumping in the Whistler area.
Day 3:
* Take a train to the city of Victoria and check into your hotel.
* Visit the Royal BC Museum and learn about the history and culture of the province.
* Take a walk through the Butchart Gardens, a large floral garden in a former quarry.
* Enjoy a performance of traditional First Nations dance at the Royal Theatre.
Day 4:
* Take a ferry to the nearby Gulf Islands and enjoy a day of exploring the islands.
* Visit the Salt Spring Island Cheese Company and try some of their products.
* Take a walk along the shore and enjoy the views.
* Return to Victoria and enjoy a farewell dinner at a restaurant in the city.
Day 5:
* Depart from Vancouver

Note: This itinerary is a rough guide and can be adjusted to suit your interests and budget. It is always recommended to check the opening hours of the places you want to visit, and book tickets in advance if possible to save time.
