
Day 1:
* Arrive in Uganda and check into a hotel in the city center
* Visit the Uganda Museum to learn about the country's history and culture
* Try some local cuisine for dinner at a restaurant in the city

Day 2:
* Visit the National Theater of Uganda and experience a traditional performance or festival
* Visit the nearby Uganda Art Gallery to see the work of local artists

Day 3:
* Take a day trip to the city of Jinja and visit the source of the Nile River
* Try some local cuisine at a restaurant in Jinja
* Visit the nearby Mabira Forest for a nature walk
* Return to Uganda and visit the Uganda Wildlife Education Center to learn about the country's wildlife

Note: This itinerary is a rough guide and can be adjusted to suit your interests and budget. It is always recommended to check the opening hours of the places you want to visit, and book tickets in advance if possible to save time.
