
Day 1:
* Arrive in Rome and check into your hotel.
* Visit the Colosseum and take a guided tour of the building.
* Walk around the Roman Forum, a large public square surrounded by important government and religious buildings.
* Visit the Pantheon, a former Roman temple that is now a church.
* Try some local cuisine at a restaurant in the city center.

Day 2:
* Take a train to Florence and check into your hotel.
* Visit the Duomo and take a guided tour of the building.
* Walk around the city center and visit some of the city's famous landmarks, like the Ponte Vecchio and the Uffizi Gallery.
* Try some local cuisine at a restaurant in the city center.

Day 3:
* Take a train to Venice and check into your hotel.
* Visit the Grand Canal and take a gondola ride.
* Walk around the city center and visit some of the city's famous landmarks, like the Rialto Bridge and the Doge's Palace.
* Try some local cuisine at a restaurant in the city center.

Day 4:
* Take a train to Pisa and check into your hotel.
* Visit the Leaning Tower of Pisa and take a guided tour of the building.
* Walk around the city center and visit some of the city's famous landmarks, like the Pisa Cathedral and the Baptistery.
* Try some local cuisine at a restaurant in the city center.
